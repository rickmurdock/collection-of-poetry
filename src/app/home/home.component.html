A COLLECTION OF
POETRY


BY

ARTHUR H. SCHILKE

Arthur H. Schilke at Lake Eola Park
in Orlando, Florida about 1940.

Poem Categories

Published

Poems that were published in the Orlando Sentinel and the Orlando Evening Star in the 1930's and early 1940's.

Worshipful

Poems related to our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ and the Word of God. Poems of worship and Praise.

General

Poems about life, love and family. Poems about home and politics. Poems about anything.

Untitled

Poems that were never given titles. Many are short. Maybe, works in progress that were never finished.
